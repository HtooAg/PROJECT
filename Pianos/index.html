Pianos

Our Grand Pianos

cfx

Crafted for the world's biggest stages. Pure sound flows effortlessly from artist to piano to audience, filling the world's most prestigious concert halls.

CF Series

This concert grand piano series excels at harmonizing with the size of the performance space and the arrangement of other instruments, drawing from its CFX inheritance and delivering sounds of sparkling brilliance and radiant color.

SX Series

This high-end series responds to the refined artistry of the player with luxurious sound and overflowing expressive power that transcends the size. Features sounds of remarkable warmth and depth, naturally elicited through Yamaha's unique Acoustic Resonance Enhancement (A.R.E.) technology.

CX Series

This standard series responds to the artistic emotions of passionate players from all over the world and in any era. It has an evolved tone and expressive feel that draws on the inheritance of the CFX, and offers the optimum size for all kinds of performance situations.

c3studio

The well-balanced size (186 cm in length) ensures a full, profound sound with a magnificent sense of harmony. Featuring all of Yamaha's uniquely reliable quality, it offers true playing joy to every player.

GC Series

With its elegant, traditional design, this grand piano series delivers the joy of playing to as many people as possible, boasting—despite its compact size—a rich sound and delicate touch that only Yamaha can offer.
